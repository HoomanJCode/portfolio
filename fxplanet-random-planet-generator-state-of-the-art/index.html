The world of NFTs continues to evolve, pushing the boundaries of digital art and creativity. One of the most exciting projects in the generative art space is FxPlanet, a 3D NFT generative collection built on the Tezos ꜩ blockchain. With a total of 2000 unique, intricately designed planets, this collection showcases the perfect blend of art, technology, and procedural generation.

The Technology Behind FxPlanet

FxPlanet is not just another NFT collection—it’s an innovative fusion of Unity and complex procedural algorithms. The project took an immense amount of time, dedication, and technical expertise to perfect, especially when it came to refining the planet textures.

At its core, the project generates planets using multiple layers of noise in a highly complex manner. This ensures that each planet is truly one-of-a-kind, with distinct patterns, colors, and surface details that make them visually stunning.

What is fxhash?

fxhash is an open generative art platform that allows artists to create and mint generative artworks as NFTs on the Tezos blockchain. Unlike traditional NFT marketplaces, fxhash is specifically designed for generative art, where each minted piece is uniquely generated using code. This makes it the perfect platform for projects like FxPlanet, where every minted planet is entirely unique and created through procedural algorithms.

Live Experience on fxhash

One of the most exciting aspects of FxPlanet is that it is fully interactive. Unlike static NFT collections, FxPlanet allows users to explore and engage with their planets in real time. You can view the live project on fxhash: 👉 FxPlanet on fxhash

Additionally, users can view random variations of planets by clicking on the Variation button under the project showcase on the fxhash page.

Controls for Exploration

To enhance the experience, users can interact with their planets using the following controls:

  • Rotate around planets: Use the WASD keys or arrow keys.
  • Zoom In: Press E.
  • Zoom Out: Press Q.
  • Change Texture Resolution:
    • Full HD (1080p): Press 1.
    • 4K Texture: Press 2.
    • 8K Texture: Press 3.

Why FxPlanet Stands Out

  • Procedural Generation: Each planet is uniquely created using complex noise layering techniques.
  • High-Quality Textures: Users can experience the planets in up to 8K resolution.
  • Interactive Experience: Unlike traditional NFT art, FxPlanet allows users to explore and interact with their planets.
  • Tezos Blockchain: Being on Tezos ensures eco-friendly minting and low transaction costs.
